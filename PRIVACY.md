# LinkEffekt — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** July 10, 2026

## Data Collection

LinkEffekt does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app does not require an account, login, or registration.

## Local Network (LAN)

LinkEffekt uses Ableton Link Audio to stream audio over your local network. This communication is peer-to-peer within your local network only. No data is sent to external servers.

## Online Mode (version 2.0 and later)

The optional Online mode streams audio over the internet between you and your peer. Audio is transmitted **peer-to-peer** and encrypted end-to-end (DTLS). It is never stored, and it never passes through our servers in readable form:

- A signaling server relays only the connection handshake (room code, connection offers, display names you enter). Rooms are temporary; empty rooms are deleted automatically.
- If a direct peer-to-peer connection is not possible, an encrypted relay (TURN) forwards the still-encrypted audio stream without being able to read or store it.
- No audio recordings, no listening history, and no message contents are retained.

## Analytics

LinkEffekt does not include any analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks.

## Purchases

The Unlimited Channels purchase is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We receive no personal payment information.

## Local Storage

LinkEffekt stores your plugin settings (channel name, preferences) locally on your device using standard iOS/macOS mechanisms (UserDefaults within an App Group). This data never leaves your device.
