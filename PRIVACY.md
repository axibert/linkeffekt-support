# LinkEffekt — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** May 6, 2026

## Data Collection

LinkEffekt does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app does not require an account, login, or registration.

## Network Usage

LinkEffekt uses Ableton Link Audio to stream audio over your local network (LAN) to Ableton Live. This communication is peer-to-peer within your local network only. No data is sent to external servers.

## Analytics

LinkEffekt does not include any analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks.

## Local Storage

LinkEffekt stores your plugin settings (channel name, preferences) locally on your device using standard iOS/macOS mechanisms (UserDefaults within an App Group). This data never leaves your device.
