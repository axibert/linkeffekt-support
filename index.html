LinkEffekt Receive Instrument

Two ways to connect

On stage or across the internet

LAN · Ableton Link Audio

  • Same Wi-Fi, zero setup: channels appear by name.
  • Low latency, adjustable from 3 ms.
  • Streams straight into Ableton Live 12.4+.
  • Beat Sync locks incoming audio onto your Link grid.

Online · rooms over the internet

  • Create a room, share the code, jam together.
  • Peer-to-peer and encrypted end-to-end (DTLS).
  • Beat Sync delays by whole bars: the peer's “1” lands on your “1”.
  • Two-way audio in a single slot with “Send my track”.
